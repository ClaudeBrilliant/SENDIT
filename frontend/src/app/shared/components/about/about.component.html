Our Story

Founded in 2020, SendIT was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to make delivery services faster, more reliable, and more accessible to everyone. What started as a small startup has grown into a trusted partner for thousands of customers worldwide.

Our journey began when our founder, Alex Johnson, experienced the frustration of unreliable delivery services while running an e-commerce business. Determined to solve this problem, he assembled a team of logistics experts and technology innovators to create a delivery service that truly puts customers first.

Today, we're proud to serve customers in over 200 countries, delivering packages with unmatched speed, security, and reliability. Our commitment to innovation and customer satisfaction drives everything we do.

Our Mission

To provide the world's most reliable, efficient, and customer-centric delivery service, making global commerce accessible to everyone through innovative technology and exceptional service.

Our Vision

To become the global standard for delivery excellence, connecting people and businesses worldwide through seamless, sustainable, and secure logistics solutions.
